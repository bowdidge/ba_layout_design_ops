    • Layout Design Challenge

    Want to show off your layout design skills, or test how well you can design for an unfamiliar space? Every year, the PCR Layout Design & Operations meet suggests a particular design problem - how to design a layout for a given space, a specific location, or a particularly challenging situation. We invite meet attendees to design a model railroad to meet the challenges, then give a short (5 minute) presentation at the meet.

    Watch this space closer to the meet to see this year's challenge, and learn how to sign up for the challenge. Both online and in-person attendees can participate; online participants will need to pre-record their presentation for showing at the meet.

    Past challenges have included:

    • Designing for a typical 10x10 bedroom in a modern house.
    • Designing a layout for the San Francisco peninsula's former Southern Pacific line.
    • Designing a layout in a British exhibition layout style
