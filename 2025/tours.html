    • Saturday Evening Layout Tours

    Interested in seeing what others have built? Come to the 2025 PCR Layout Design and Operations Weekend, where you'll be able to tour local layouts on Saturday evening.

    Layouts will be in the Sacramento and Roseville area. Lists of layouts will be provided to attendees close to the event. Carpool with other attendees to make new friends and talk about what you like about each layout!

    Contact Jim Radkey (sscotty@att.net) if you'd like to have your layout open during the Saturday evening layout tours.
