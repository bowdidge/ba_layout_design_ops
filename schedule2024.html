Schedule for 2024 Bay Area Layout Design and Operations meet

This is the official schedule for the 2024 Bay Area Layout Design and Operations meet in Santa Rosa.

Schedule is subject to change, please check back closer to the event for changes.

Friday, February 2: Tour, Virtual Meet-up, and Dinner

2:30 pm - 4:30 pm: Tour of Northwestern Pacific Railroad Historical Society library, 75 Magnolia Ave., Petaluma CA 94952 (map).

Visit the Northwestern Pacific Historical Society's archive. The NWP Historical Society Archives and Library is the site where volunteer staff catalog and study documents, photos, plans and blueprints, and other material for the purpose of preserving the history and heritage of this legendary line, from its origins to the present.

Meet in front of the library at 75 Magnolia Ave. in Petaluma at 2:30 pm. You'll be guided through the library, seeing the various worksites and materials involved in preserving railroad history. Attendees may go through in smaller groups depending on space inside the museum.

5:00 pm - 6:00 pm: Virtual Check-in on Zoom

Meet your fellow attendees and organizers on a virtual get-together on Zoom. Meet some of our panel participants, organizers, modelers and operators for a meet and greet before Saturday's event. We will bring you photos, video and information from our tour at Northwestern Pacific Historical Society Library in Petaluma, California. We will also have technical reminders, help you verify your setup and connection,and some housekeeping notes.

6:00 pm - 8:00 pm: Group dinner at Chevy's, Railroad Square, 24 4th Street, Santa Rosa CA 95401 (map).

Meet your fellow in-person attendees for a group buffet dinner at the Chevy's restaurant in the old Petaluma and Santa Rosa depot in Santa Rosa's Railroad Square Historic District. Dinner is $42 for the meal and non-alcoholic drinks, or get alcoholic drinks at the bar. Buy your ticket for dinner at the meet registration site on EventBrite. For questions about registering for dinner, contact Cydney Abatecola (cgeorge1314@gmail.com).
