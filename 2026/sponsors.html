    Pacific Coast Region of National Model Railroad Association (PCR NMRA)
    The Pacific Coast Region is the local organization bringing together model railroaders across Northern California, Oregon, Nevada, and Hawaii. We arrange frequent meets throughout the region, hold a yearly convention, and hold multiple virtual events every year.
    Layout Design Special Interest Group
    The Layout Design Special Interest Group (LDSIG) is a non-profit educational institution that exists to further the art of model railroad track planning and model train layout design. In addition to its regular publication (the Layout Design Journal), the group also organizes layout design-related events at NMRA conventions and meetings, and holds virtual and in-person events to share layout design advice.
