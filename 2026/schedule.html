    • Schedule for 2026 PCR Layout Design and Operations meet

    Saturday, January 31: Operating Sessions, Dinner, and Virtual Meet-up

    9:00 am - 5:00 pm: Operating sessions at nearby layouts.

    Operating sessions are by invitation - learn more about participating in an operating session during the meet.

    6:00 pm - 8:00: Group no-host dinner at Empire Armadillo BBQ & Deli, 161 East El Camino Real, Sunnyvale.

    Meet your fellow in-person attendees for a group no-host dinner at Empire Armadillo (formerly Armadillo Willy's) barbecue restaurant.

    We'll have a reserved set of tables inside the restaurant; order at the counter and pay separately.

    Sunday, February 1: Clinics and Layout Tours

    8:45 am - 5:15 pm: Clinics at South Bay Railroad Historical Society (Santa Clara Caltrain Station), 1005 Railroad Ave., Santa Clara CA 95050. (map)
    8:15 am Doors open.
    8:45 - 9:00 am Welcome, introduction, news. (Robert Bowdidge)
    9:00 - 9:50 am The San Francisco State Belt Railway, Saint Paul division (David Zuhn) (handout)
    10:00 - 10:50 am How our operators changed how we operate the Monterey Branch (Dave Loveless, Charlie Bedard, Tommy Tomasello)
    11:00 - 11:50 am The Role of the Agent - and how we can model it (Tony Thompson)
    12:00 - 1:15 pm Lunch nearby - on your own.
    1:15 - 1:30 pm OPSIG Achievement Award (Tony Thompson)
    1:30 - 2:10 pm Car orders on the Central Vermont in NorCal (Jon Schmidt)
    2:10 - 2:50 pm Car routing on the Central Vermont (Jim Providenza)
    2:50 - 3:30 pm The Central Vermont: A clerk's view (Michael Litant)
    3:30 - 3:45 pm Break
    3:45 - 5:00 pm Layout Design Challenge: Lentzen Street engine terminal (Bruce Morden and participants)
    5:00 - 5:15 pm Wrap-up, thanks (Robert Bowdidge)
    9:00 - 2:00 pm: Layout Design and Operations consultations at the South Bay Railroad Historical Society.

    Sign up for a consultation session to get advice on your model railroad layout design or operations scheme from an experienced modeler. Learn more about signing up for a design consulation.
