    • Schedule for 2026 PCR Layout Design and Operations meet

    Saturday, January 31: Operating Sessions, Layout Tours, Dinner, and Virtual Meet-up

    9:00 am - 5:00 pm: Operating sessions at nearby layouts.

    Operating sessions are by invitation - learn more about participating in an operating session during the meet.

    11:00 am - 3:00 pm: Layout tours

    Not interested in joining an operating session? We'll also have layout tours in the South Bay area, and the chance to stop by some of the operating sessions to see what it's all about. Watch your e-mail the week before the meet for the list of open layouts.

    5:00 pm - 6:00 pm: Virtual Check-in on Zoom

    Meet your fellow attendees and organizers on a virtual get-together on Zoom. Meet some of our panel participants, organizers, modelers and operators for a meet and greet before Sunday's clinics.

    6:00 pm: Group no-host dinner at nearby restaurant.

    Meet your fellow in-person attendees for a group no-host dinner. We'll have a dedicated set of tables; order and pay separately.

    Restaurant to be announced closer to the event.

    Sunday, February 1: Clinics and Layout Tours

    8:45 am - 4:45 pm: Clinics at South Bay Railroad Historical Society (Santa Clara Caltrain Station), 1005 Railroad Ave., Santa Clara CA 95050. (map)

    Check back for the full schedule.

    9:00 - 2:00 pm: Layout Design and Operations consultations at the South Bay Railroad Historical Society.

    Sign up for a consultation session to get advice on your model railroad layout design or operations scheme from an experienced modeler. Learn more about signing up for a design consulation.
